# Task 12 - Touch target audit

Source: ideas/architectural-review-2026/04-ui-ux-improvements.md

Goal
- Ensure all interactive controls meet a 48px minimum touch target.

Scope
- Audit shared buttons in styles.css and game-specific CSS files.
- Update sizing for any small buttons or icon-only controls.
- Verify hover and active states remain intact.

Acceptance criteria
- Buttons and tappable elements meet or exceed 48px on mobile.
- No overlaps or layout shifts across breakpoints.
